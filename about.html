I am a third year PhD student at Swansea University. I study gender in the online extreme right, and I do this by scraping and crawling extremist websites and analysing the textual content I find there.

As part of this research, I produce maps of these websites, some of which can be seen on the "Research" page of this website. I use Python to crawl and scrape these websites, and then I visualise the graphs created in Gephi.
